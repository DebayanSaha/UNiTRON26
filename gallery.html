Gallery FAQs

What does the Geekonix gallery include?

The gallery features EDGE event highlights including team moments, stage sessions, and participant snapshots from Techno Main Salt Lake.

Can students use these gallery photos for sharing?

Yes, students can share photos with proper context and credit to Geekonix and EDGE where applicable.

How often is the gallery updated?

The gallery is updated around fest cycles and key Geekonix activities when new event visuals are published.
